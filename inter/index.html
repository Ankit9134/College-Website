Welome To Pt. Jatashankar Ramdas Inter College

"Pt. Jatashankar Ramdas Inter College was established on 15th July 2005 in Pilkathua,Barasathi,Near Katwar railway crossing ,Jaunpur-222162 which occupies two campus covering lush green play ground with modern sports facilities."

Create effective interface with competition and community to impart "Quality Education" at par with the best in the country.

Just as greatness, so the same could be said of leadership. The world belongs to its great leaders and it comes to those who seek. At Pt. Jatashankar Ramdas Inter College, we believe that every child is a born leader

About us View All

To develop a positive, vibrant and healthy learning environment so that ideas can be converted in to constant never ending actions towards the advancement of the society.

The school is founded in the memory of Late Pt. Jatashankar Shukla and Ramdas Shukla, a noted humanitarian and philanthropist. Situated amid a picturesque and spacious campus at Pilkathua, Jaunpur-222162, it was started with just about 200 students and 8 teachers in the year 2005. Today with over 2500 students and more than 40 well qualified teachers, the main branch of the school is affiliated to Council for the Uttar Pradesh State Board of High School and Intermediate Education Prayagraj (X & XII).

While the other academic wing of pt.Jatasankar ramdas inter college which is known as 'MCS Academy' is affliated to Central Board of Secondary Education New Delhi.

While playing adequate emphasis on the prescribed syllabus, the school pays extra attention to personality development, which is furthered by involving all students in various arts, cultural, and sport activities through trained professionals.
